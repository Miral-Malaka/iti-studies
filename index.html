Institutional Overview

The Independent Thought Institute for Strategic Studies (ITISS) is a global multi-disciplinary think tank dedicated to the improvement of security and governance transitions in stable, fragile, and post-conflict environments.

Headquartered in Gaza, ITISS maintains a global network of researchers, practitioners, and academics specializing in the intersection of national security, international relations, and economic development.

Our Core Mission

Rigorous Research

Conducting evidence-based analysis on regional security trends and governance models.

Global Dialogue

Building bridges between local researchers and international policy stakeholders.

Security Transition

Aiding donor effectiveness in supporting security sector reforms in conflict zones.
